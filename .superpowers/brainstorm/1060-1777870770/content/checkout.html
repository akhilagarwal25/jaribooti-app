Checkout approach

How should customers pay?

A

Shopify checkout (Recommended)

App shows products + cart. On checkout, open Shopify's hosted checkout page in a WebView or browser. Handles payment, addresses, order confirmation. Simpler to build, Shopify manages payments.

B

Native in-app checkout

Full custom checkout within the app. Integrates Razorpay/UPI/cards directly. More complex to build but smoother UX and works without Shopify's hosted page.
