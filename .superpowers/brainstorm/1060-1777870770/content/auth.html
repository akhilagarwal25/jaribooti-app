Customer login

How should customers sign in?

A

Guest (no login needed)

No account needed. Browse, add to cart, checkout as guest. Shopify captures email/phone at checkout. Simpler for one-time buyers.

B

Shopify account login

Customers create account via Shopify. Login with email/password or OTP. Access order history, saved addresses, wishlist.

C

Google + Phone OTP

Login with Google OAuth or phone OTP (like Andha Pansari app). No password needed. Faster, familiar pattern.
